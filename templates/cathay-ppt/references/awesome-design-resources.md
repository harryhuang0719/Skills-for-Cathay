# Awesome Design Resources

Curated design resources for improving visual quality in PPT decks, UI/UX work, and all design-adjacent tasks.
Source: [awesome-design](https://github.com/gztchan/awesome-design)

## When to Use

Reference these resources when:
- Choosing color palettes for decks or dashboards
- Selecting typography combinations
- Looking for icons, illustrations, or stock imagery
- Seeking layout and visual inspiration
- Checking design patterns and best practices
- Prototyping or mocking up interfaces

## Design Principles for Investment Decks

Based on IB best practices and design resources:

1. **Visual Hierarchy**: Title (20-22pt) → Subtitle (10-11pt) → Body (9-10pt) → Caption (7-8pt)
2. **Color Restraint**: Max 3-4 colors per slide. Brand primary + accent + neutral grey + white
3. **White Space**: Don't fill every pixel. Strategic white space creates breathing room
4. **Consistency**: Same grid, margins, font sizes across all slides
5. **Data Visualization**: Tables > paragraphs for financial data. Charts for trends.
6. **Shape Language**: Rounded rectangles for callouts, circles for KPIs, thin lines for dividers
7. **Icon Usage**: Small (4-5mm) monochrome icons as section markers. Large (12mm) numbered circles for major sections
8. **Contrast**: Dark text on light bg, white text on dark bg. Never low-contrast combos
